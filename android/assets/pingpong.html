Scoreboard

Modes

  • Ping Pong — 2 players, first to N (11/15/21) with a 2-point lead wins, serve alternates.
  • General — 2–6 players, free-form scoring. Optional celebrate target.
  • Spades — 2 teams, tap + Round to enter books called then books made; standard scoring with overs (−100 per 10 overs). Race to 500.
  • Darts — 2 players, start at 301/501/701 and count down to zero. Tap 🎯 Enter dart to pick the number, then Single / Double / Triple. Bust reverts the turn.

Scoring input

  • Tap a player's card → +1 point
  • Swipe up → +1 · swipe down → −1
  • Voice: tap 🎙, then say things like blue point, another point for red, player three plus one, team two minus ten.
